Our Foundation

The Story of Pencil Tip

Established with the dream of creating an inspiring sanctuary for artistic experimentation, classical technique, and creative innovation.

Our Genesis

Bridging the Classical Atelier with Modern Expression

Pencil Tip Academy of Art was founded in 2011 by a small circle of exhibited oil painters, clay sculptors, and designers who felt standard academic education lacked the personalized, organic mentorship needed for a true artistic voice to unfold.

We started in a refurbished brick warehouse with five easels and a single potter's wheel. Over the years, we have preserved that initial raw, passionate, and hands-on atelier environment while expanding our curriculum to cover modern digital illustration and mixed media collages.

"Art does not reproduce what we see. Rather, it makes us see." — Paul Klee

Our Mission

To provide structured, high-caliber artistic mentoring that bridges the gap between raw imagination and professional, museum-level execution. We ensure every student gains fundamental accuracy in proportions, value mapping, and light behavior so their creativity can thrive without boundary.

Our Vision

To stand as an international center of artistic excellence where traditional craftsmanship is celebrated and evolved alongside digital design, establishing an inclusive community that fosters cultural reflection, conceptual risk-taking, and professional career growth.

Enrollment Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need previous drawing experience to enroll?

+

Not at all! We offer core beginner modules (e.g. Classical Sketching & Watercolor basics) designed specifically to take you from absolute zero skills to confident visual creations.

Are painting tools and sketching materials provided?

+

Yes, all basic studio items (canvases, clay stoneware, watercolor palettes, digital tablet displays) are fully accessible for use in the classroom. Students can optionally purchase a custom take-home brush kit.

Can I book custom private tutoring sessions?

+

Absolutely! We offer 1-on-1 portfolio review preparation, master mentoring, and flexible timing packages for private lessons. Contact us directly to configure your plan.

Are classes held physically, digitally, or both?

+

Classes are held physically in our natural-light NY studio to ensure authentic hands-on guidance. However, we also provide live Zoom streaming options for digital art modules.
