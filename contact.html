Quick Answers

Visiting & Hours FAQ

When are the studios open for public visits?

+

We host guided tours every Tuesday and Thursday at 4 PM, and hold open studios on Saturdays from 11 AM to 3 PM. Booking an appointment via our form is recommended.

Is there parking space near the Brooklyn warehouse?

+

Yes, street parking is available along Terracotta Blvd, and there is a commercial parking garage situated exactly two blocks north on Art District Lane.

How long does the enrollment process take?

+

Once you submit your application form, our advisors review standard course slots and send out confirmation responses within 2 to 3 business days.
