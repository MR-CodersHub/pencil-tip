Our Method

Classical Mechanics

Every masterpiece starts with technical discipline. Students master optical perspective, values, anatomy, and paint medium chemistry prior to conceptual execution.

Your Roadmap

The Artist Timeline

Phase 1: Foundational Sketching

Develop critical drawing accuracy, mapping negative space, volume grids, and anatomy lines with graphite and charcoal bars.

Phase 2: Color Chemistry & Viscosity

Study color temperatures, light reflections, mineral pigments, paint glazes, and medium drying rates on wood canvases.

Phase 3: Group Critiques & Mentorship

Gain direct, structured feedback from exhibited artists. Refine layout compositions and thematic concepts inside the classroom.

Phase 4: Public Gallery Exhibition

Curate your final portfolio collection, participate in public virtual museum previews, and coordinate physical gallery open-nights.
